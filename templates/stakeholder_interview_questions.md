## 2. Current Process Questions

1. Can you describe the current recruitment workflow?
2. What tools do you use to manage candidates today?
3. What information do you collect from candidates?
4. How do you track candidate status?
5. How do you schedule interviews?
6. How do interviewers submit feedback?
7. How is the final hiring decision made?

## 3. Pain Point Questions

1. Which step takes the most time?
2. Which step causes the most confusion?
3. What information is difficult to find?
4. Where do errors usually happen?
5. What causes delays in the recruitment process?

## 4. Business Rule Questions

1. Who can update candidate status?
2. Who can schedule interviews?
3. Who can view candidate CVs?
4. Who can approve final hiring decisions?
5. What status changes should be allowed?
6. What candidate data should be protected?

## 5. Future Process Questions

1. What should the new system improve first?
2. What features are must-have for the first version?
3. What reports or dashboard do you need?
4. What notifications should candidates receive?
5. What would make this project successful?
